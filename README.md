# European Soccer Match Prediction & Analytics Dashboard

This project presents a comprehensive Sales Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze customer behavior, product performance, profitability, and discount impact for a retail store.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

### üìå Key Insights

#### Business Growth
Sales and profit show a sustained upward trend over the analyzed period (2011‚Äì2014), indicating business stability and growth.

#### Customer Analysis

- The Medium Frequency segment represents 60.8% of total revenue.
- A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total profit.
- High purchase frequency customers are limited but strategically important.

#### Product Performance

- Profit peaks are observed in June, September, and November.
- Lower profitability months correlate with higher discount levels.
- Product-level contribution varies significantly, suggesting optimization opportunities.

#### Discount Impact
The dashboard includes a discount simulation tool, demonstrating the direct relationship between discount percentage and profit margin.
